Efficient Automatic Depth Estimation for Video
Supplemental Results [10.1109/ICDSP.2013.6622807]

On this page you'll find the supplemental results for "Efficient Automatic Depth Estimation for Video". These are the full-resolution versions of the results presented in the paper. Also included are the videos of the frame sequences presented at the end of the paper in the results section.

Depth Interpolation

The interpolation section has two sets of results. The first is a comparison between the old interpolation method1 and the proposed one. The second set is an example of how the depth map can be adjusted by a user.
